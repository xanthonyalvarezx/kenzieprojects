Philadelphia

The city of brotherly love

I grew up in philadelphia. I lived in philadelphia for twenty-one years, before moving to Bucks County to live with my now wife and in-laws.

I love my city and all the memories I have of venturing downtown, or in Philadelphian, Center City as we call it. The street of Center city really raised me as a teen if I'm going to be completely honest. It's one of thoes places where you can be completely in peace. The Smell of food carts on the corner. The sound of the traffic, and the feeling of the world speeding past as you're standing still.

My favorite things to do in Philadelphia:


  • South street

  • Repo Records

  • One Liberty observation deck

  • The philadelphia art museam

  • The Bourse building

  • Penns Landing
